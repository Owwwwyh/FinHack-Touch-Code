# TNG Digital FINHACK 2026

## Judging Criteria

### Overview

Judging criteria for **Preliminary Round** and **Grand Final** will be the same.

## 1. AI & Intelligent Systems

**Effective and meaningful integration of Artificial Intelligence to address the problem statements**

## 2. Technical Implementation

**Scalability, robustness, security or prototype**

## 3. Multi-Cloud Service Usage

**Effective and purposeful use of at least two or more cloud platforms (Alibaba Cloud & AWS)**

## 4. Impact & Feasibility

**Real-world use case relevance, sustainability and potential adoption**

## 5. Presentation & Teamwork

**Clarity in project demo and pitch, teamwork, documentation quality**

## Participant Project Submission

### Deliverables

Participants are expected to submit the following deliverables by the end of TNG Digital Finhack:

1. **Team Name**
2. **Project Name, Description, Track Submitted**
3. **Implementation & Inspiration**
4. **Pitch Deck Link**
5. **Demo Video Link**
6. **Deployment Link**
7. **Github Repository Link**

## Focus Areas

**Select ONE focus area for your project:**

#### 1. Financial Inclusion (our choice)
